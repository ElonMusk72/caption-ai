Privacy Policy for CaptionAI

Last Updated: April 20, 2026

1. No Data Collection

CaptionAI does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users.

2. How Our App Works

When you upload an image to generate a caption, the image is sent directly to Google Gemini API for processing. We do not store your images on our servers. Your data is used solely to generate the requested caption.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google Gemini API to generate captions. By using CaptionAI, you acknowledge that your image may be processed by Google's servers. Please review Google's privacy policy for more information.

4. No Account Required

You do not need to create an account or provide any personal information to use CaptionAI.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.
